Outgoing webhook handling is in `server/polar/webhook/service.py`, `server/polar/webhook/webhooks.py`, `server/polar/webhook/tasks.py`, and `server/polar/webhook/repository.py`. Specifically: `service.py` selects enabled endpoints/events and enqueues sends, `webhooks.py` serializes raw/Discord/Slack payloads, `tasks.py` signs, posts, records delivery, retries, and enforces FIFO delays, backed by `repository.py`’s earlier-pending count.
