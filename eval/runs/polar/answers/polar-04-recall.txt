A subscription renewal is orchestrated in [server/polar/subscription/tasks.py](/Users/knrz/Git/CyrusNuevoDia/capn-hook/eval/runs/work/polar-capn/server/polar/subscription/tasks.py), where the `subscription.cycle` actor loads and locks due subscriptions, then calls `subscription_service.cycle`. The core renewal logic lives in [server/polar/subscription/service.py](/Users/knrz/Git/CyrusNuevoDia/capn-hook/eval/runs/work/polar-capn/server/polar/subscription/service.py): it applies pending updates, advances billing periods, enqueues renewal order creation, and cycles or revokes benefits.
