The subject is resolved in `server/polar/auth/middlewares.py`: `get_auth_subject()` maps bearer tokens or the user session cookie into an `AuthSubject` for `User`, `Organization`, `Customer`, `Member`, or `Anonymous`. The required subject type and scopes are enforced in `server/polar/auth/dependencies.py` by `Authenticator`; `server/polar/auth/models.py` defines the `AuthSubject` model and subject types.
