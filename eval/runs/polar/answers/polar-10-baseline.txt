Merchant-of-record tax is calculated in `server/polar/checkout/service.py` (`_update_checkout_tax`) during checkout and in `server/polar/order/service.py` (`_calculate_tax`) for order/subscription creation flows. It is recorded from `server/polar/order/service.py` via `tax_calculation_service.record`, reverted on refunds in `server/polar/refund/service.py`, and the processor dispatch/backfill logic lives in `server/polar/tax/calculation/__init__.py` with provider implementations under `server/polar/tax/calculation/`.
