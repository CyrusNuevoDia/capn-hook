The worker entrypoint is `server/polar/subscription/tasks.py`, where the `subscription.cycle` actor loads and locks the subscription before calling the service. The renewal behavior itself is in `server/polar/subscription/service.py`: `SubscriptionService.cycle()` applies pending updates, advances billing periods, enqueues `order.create_subscription_order`, and uses `enqueue_benefits_grants()` to grant, revoke, or seat-revoke benefits.
