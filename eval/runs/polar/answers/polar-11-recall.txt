Seller payout eligibility lives in `server/polar/models/organization.py` and `server/polar/models/payout_account.py`; Stripe/manual payout account state is handled in `server/polar/payout_account/service.py`. Payout creation/transfer/cancellation orchestration is in `server/polar/payout/service.py` and `server/polar/payout/tasks.py`, while ledger payout transactions and reversals are created in `server/polar/transaction/service/payout.py`.
