Recipient document completion is validated in `packages/lib/server-only/document/complete-document-with-token.ts`, with signing-order delegated to `packages/lib/server-only/recipient/get-is-recipient-turn.ts` and access-auth derivation in `packages/lib/utils/document-auth.ts`; the TRPC entry point is `packages/trpc/server/recipient-router/router.ts`. The CSC/TSP completion path is handled separately in `packages/ee/server-only/signing/csc/execute-tsp-sign.ts`, which mirrors the audit-log, webhook, and follow-up job side effects.
