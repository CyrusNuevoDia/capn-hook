Documenso webhooks are triggered via `packages/lib/server-only/webhooks/trigger/trigger-webhook.ts`, with event call sites in the document/envelope/template/signing job files like `send-document.ts`, `complete-document-with-token.ts`, `seal-document.handler.ts`, and `execute-tsp-sign.ts`. They are executed and persisted in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/internal/execute-webhook.handler.ts` plus `packages/lib/server-only/webhooks/execute-webhook-call.ts`, stored by the `Webhook`/`WebhookCall` models in `packages/prisma/schema.prisma`, and manually resent from `apps/remix/app/components/general/webhook-logs-sheet.tsx` through `packages/trpc/server/webhook-router/resend-webhook-call.ts`.
