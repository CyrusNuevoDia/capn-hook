Documenso webhooks are triggered via `packages/lib/server-only/webhooks/trigger/trigger-webhook.ts`, which queues `internal.execute-webhook`; that job is defined in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/internal/execute-webhook.ts`, executed/logged in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/internal/execute-webhook.handler.ts`, and the actual POST happens in `packages/lib/server-only/webhooks/execute-webhook-call.ts`.

Webhook configs and call logs are persisted through the Prisma `Webhook`/`WebhookCall` models in `packages/prisma/schema.prisma` plus `packages/lib/server-only/webhooks/create-webhook.ts`/`edit-webhook.ts`, and manual resend is exposed in `apps/remix/app/components/general/webhook-logs-sheet.tsx` and handled by `packages/trpc/server/webhook-router/resend-webhook-call.ts`.
