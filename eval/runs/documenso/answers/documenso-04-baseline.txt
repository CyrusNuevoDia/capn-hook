A signer’s submitted value is handled in `packages/trpc/server/envelope-router/sign-envelope-field.ts`: it calls `extractFieldInsertionValues` in `packages/lib/utils/envelope-signing.ts` for per-field validation, `validateFieldAuth` in `packages/lib/server-only/document/validate-field-auth.ts` for signature-field auth, then saves `Field.customText`/`Field.inserted`, upserts `Signature`, and writes `DocumentAuditLog`. The actual tables/columns are defined in `packages/prisma/schema.prisma` under `Field`, `Signature`, and `DocumentAuditLog`; the older/non-v2 token path mirrors this in `packages/lib/server-only/field/sign-field-with-token.ts`.
