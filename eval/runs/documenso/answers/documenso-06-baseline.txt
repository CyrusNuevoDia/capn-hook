After distribution, `packages/lib/server-only/document/send-document.ts` triggers the `send.signing.requested.email` job for each recipient to notify. The email is assembled and sent in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/emails/send-signing-email.handler.ts`, using the React email templates in `packages/email/templates/document-invite.tsx` and `packages/email/template-components/template-document-invite.tsx`.
