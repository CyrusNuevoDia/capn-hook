Expired recipients are swept in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/internal/expire-recipients-sweep.ts` and `expire-recipients-sweep.handler.ts`, then processed in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/internal/process-recipient-expired.handler.ts`. Owner notification is sent via `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/emails/send-owner-recipient-expired-email.handler.ts`, and webhook emission goes through `packages/lib/server-only/webhooks/trigger/trigger-webhook.ts`.
