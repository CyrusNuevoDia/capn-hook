Expired recipients are detected by the cron job in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/internal/expire-recipients-sweep.ts` and selected/enqueued in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/internal/expire-recipients-sweep.handler.ts`. They are processed in `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/internal/process-recipient-expired.handler.ts`, which marks `expirationNotifiedAt`, writes the audit log, emits `RECIPIENT_EXPIRED` via `packages/lib/server-only/webhooks/trigger/trigger-webhook.ts`, and queues the owner email sent by `packages/lib/jobs/definitions/emails/send-owner-recipient-expired-email.handler.ts`.
