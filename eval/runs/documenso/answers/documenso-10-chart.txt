Paid subscription checkout is started in `packages/trpc/server/enterprise-router/create-subscription.ts`, via `createSubscriptionRoute` calling `createCheckoutSession`. A paid organisation creation path also starts checkout in `packages/trpc/server/organisation-router/create-organisation.ts`; both use `packages/ee/server-only/stripe/create-checkout-session.ts` to call Stripe with `mode: 'subscription'`.
