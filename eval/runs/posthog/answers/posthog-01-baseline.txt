For normal analytics events, Rust runs `rust/capture/src/events/analytics.rs::process_events`, which hands `ProcessedEvent`s to `rust/capture/src/sinks/kafka.rs::KafkaSinkBase::prepare_record/send(_batch)` and produces to `events_plugin_ingestion`; Node consumes that via `nodejs/src/ingestion/pipelines/analytics/joined-ingestion-pipeline.ts`, reaches `event-subpipeline.ts` and `nodejs/src/ingestion/common/steps/event-processing/emit-event-step.ts::serializeEvent`, then emits `EVENTS_OUTPUT` configured in `nodejs/src/ingestion/pipelines/analytics/outputs/registry.ts` to `clickhouse_events_json`.

ClickHouse consumes that final topic through `posthog/models/event/sql.py`: `KAFKA_EVENTS_TABLE_JSON_SQL()` / `KAFKA_EVENTS_TABLE_JSON_WS_SQL()` define the Kafka-engine table schema, and `EVENTS_TABLE_JSON_MV_SQL()` materializes it into `writable_events`.
