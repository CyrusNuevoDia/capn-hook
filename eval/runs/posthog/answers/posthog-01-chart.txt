For normal analytics capture, `rust/capture/src/events/analytics.rs` turns the request into `ProcessedEvent`s and calls the Kafka sink, while `rust/capture/src/sinks/kafka.rs` serializes them and routes `DataType::AnalyticsMain` to `events_plugin_ingestion`; Node then consumes that in `nodejs/src/ingestion/pipelines/analytics/joined-ingestion-pipeline.ts` and `nodejs/src/ingestion/common/steps/event-processing/emit-event-step.ts` serializes the ClickHouse row to the `events` output, wired in `nodejs/src/ingestion/config.ts` / `nodejs/src/common/config/kafka-topics.ts` as `clickhouse_events_json`.

The final Kafka-engine schema is in `posthog/models/event/sql.py`: `KAFKA_EVENTS_TABLE_JSON_SQL()` defines `kafka_events_json`, and `KAFKA_EVENTS_TABLE_JSON_WS_SQL()` defines the WarpStream equivalent `kafka_events_json_ws`, both consuming `clickhouse_events_json` before the materialized view writes into the events table.
