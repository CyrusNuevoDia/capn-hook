The record index table path is in `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/object-record/record-index/`: `useLoadRecordIndexStates.ts` loads current view metadata into state, `useFindManyRecordIndexTableParams.ts` turns it into query params, and `RecordIndexTableContainer.tsx` passes it into the table. The table wrapper and per-cell field context live under `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/object-record/record-table/`: `RecordTableWithWrappers.tsx`, then `RecordTableCellWrapper.tsx`, `RecordTableCellFieldContextWrapper.tsx`, and `RecordTableCellFieldContextGeneric.tsx`.
