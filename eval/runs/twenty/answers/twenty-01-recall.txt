Adding a custom field starts in `packages/twenty-front/src/pages/settings/data-model/new-field/SettingsObjectNewFieldConfigure.tsx`, then flows through `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/object-metadata/hooks/useFieldMetadataItem.ts` and `useCreateOneFieldMetadataItem.ts` into `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/metadata-modules/field-metadata/field-metadata.resolver.ts` and `services/field-metadata.service.ts`.

The flat-field expansion is handled by `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/metadata-modules/flat-field-metadata/utils/from-create-field-input-to-flat-field-metadatas-to-create.util.ts`, with relation-specific expansion in `from-relation-create-field-input-to-flat-field-metadatas.util.ts` and morph relation expansion in `from-morph-relation-create-field-input-to-flat-field-metadatas.util.ts`.
