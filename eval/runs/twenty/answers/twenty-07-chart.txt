Switching an existing billing plan is handled by `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/settings/billing/hooks/useSwitchBillingPlan.ts` via `SwitchBillingPlanDocument` from `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/settings/billing/graphql/mutations/switchSubscriptionToEnterprisePlan.ts`, then `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/billing/billing.resolver.ts` calls `BillingSubscriptionUpdateService.changePlan` in `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/billing/services/billing-subscription-update.service.ts`.

For Stripe checkout/new subscription plan selection, the path is `BillingResolver.checkoutSession` -> `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/billing/services/billing-portal.workspace-service.ts` -> `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/billing/stripe/services/stripe-checkout.service.ts`.
