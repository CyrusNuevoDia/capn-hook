Gmail import is charted across `packages/twenty-server/src/modules/messaging/message-import-manager/drivers/gmail/services/gmail-get-message-list.service.ts`, `gmail-get-messages.service.ts`, and `utils/compute-gmail-exclude-search-filter.util.ts`, then the generic import pipeline in `services/messaging-message-list-fetch.service.ts`, `messaging-get-message-list.service.ts`, `messaging-get-messages.service.ts`, `messaging-messages-import.service.ts`, `messaging-save-messages-and-enqueue-contact-creation.service.ts`, and `messaging-message.service.ts`. In short: the Gmail list fetch builds the excluded-label query, the import service fetches full messages, and the save services persist messages/threads/associations.
