Manual command-menu triggering is handled in `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/command-menu-item/hooks/useCommandMenuItemClick.ts`, `.../engine-command/hooks/useMountCommand.ts`, `.../hooks/useEnrichHeadlessCommandContextApiWithWorkflowVersionTriggerInformation.ts`, `.../record/components/TriggerWorkflowVersionEngineCommand.tsx`, `.../utils/buildTriggerWorkflowVersionPayloads.ts`, then `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/workflow/hooks/useRunWorkflowVersion.tsx` calls the GraphQL mutation in `.../graphql/mutations/runWorkflowVersion.ts`.

On the server, GraphQL enters at `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/workflow/resolvers/workflow-trigger.resolver.ts`, REST/webhook triggering enters at `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/workflow/controllers/workflow-trigger.controller.ts`, both call `packages/twenty-server/src/modules/workflow/workflow-trigger/workspace-services/workflow-trigger.workspace-service.ts`, which delegates to `packages/twenty-server/src/modules/workflow/workflow-runner/workspace-services/workflow-runner.workspace-service.ts` to enqueue `RunWorkflowJob`; non-manual runs go through `workflow-run-queue/jobs/workflow-run-enqueue.job.ts` and `workflow-run-queue/workspace-services/workflow-run-enqueue.workspace-service.ts`.
