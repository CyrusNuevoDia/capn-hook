Selected GraphQL fields are parsed in `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/api/graphql/graphql-query-runner/graphql-query-parsers/graphql-query.parser.ts` and the `graphql-query-selected-fields` parsers. They’re turned into DB select/return columns by `utils/build-columns-to-select.ts` and `utils/build-columns-to-return.ts`, then applied in `common-query-runners/common-find-many-query-runner.service.ts`.
