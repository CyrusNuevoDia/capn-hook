The per-workspace schema is assembled under `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/api/graphql/workspace-schema-builder/`: `workspace-graphql-schema.factory.ts` creates the final `GraphQLSchema`, while `graphql-type-generators/gql-type.generator.ts` orchestrates object metadata into generated types. The specific query-root and type builders are `graphql-type-generators/root-types/root-type.generator.ts`, `root-types/query-type.generator.ts`, `object-types/object-metadata-gql-object-type.generator.ts`, and `input-types/object-metadata-gql-input-type.generator.ts`.
