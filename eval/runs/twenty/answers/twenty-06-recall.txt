The SSO sign-in flow is handled in `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/auth/sign-in-up/hooks/useSSO.ts`, then through `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/auth/controllers/sso-auth.controller.ts` and `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/auth/services/auth-sso.service.ts`.
