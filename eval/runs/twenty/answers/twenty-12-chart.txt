The single-record picker search starts in `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/object-record/record-picker/single-record-picker/hooks/useSingleRecordPickerPerformSearch.ts`, which calls the shared `packages/twenty-front/src/modules/object-record/hooks/useObjectRecordSearchRecords.ts` hook and its `SearchDocument` query. That lands in `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/search/search.resolver.ts`, which delegates the actual search work to `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/core-modules/search/services/search.service.ts`.
