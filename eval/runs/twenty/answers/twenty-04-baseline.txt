Selected fields are read in the dynamic resolver factories like `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/api/graphql/workspace-resolver-builder/factories/find-many-resolver.factory.ts`, then parsed in `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/api/graphql/graphql-query-runner/graphql-query-parsers/graphql-query-selected-fields/graphql-selected-fields.parser.ts`.

They are turned into DB projections in `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/api/graphql/graphql-query-runner/utils/build-columns-to-select.ts` and applied by the common record runners such as `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/api/common/common-query-runners/common-find-many-query-runner.service.ts`; the return-column helper is `packages/twenty-server/src/engine/api/graphql/graphql-query-runner/utils/build-columns-to-return.ts`.
