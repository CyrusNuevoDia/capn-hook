Manual workflow-version triggering is charted across: `TriggerWorkflowVersionEngineCommand.tsx`, `buildTriggerWorkflowVersionPayloads.ts`, `runWorkflowVersion.ts`, and `useRunWorkflowVersion.tsx` on the frontend, then `workflow-trigger.resolver.ts` for GraphQL and `workflow-trigger.controller.ts` for REST. Both paths call `workflow-trigger.workspace-service.ts`, which delegates to `workflow-runner.workspace-service.ts` to create an `ENQUEUED` manual `workflowRun` and queue `run-workflow.job.ts`.
