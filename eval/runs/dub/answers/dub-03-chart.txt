Aggregate `/api/analytics` requests enter through `apps/web/app/api/analytics/route.ts`, which parses/normalizes the request and calls `getAnalytics`. The actual translation into Tinybird pipe calls happens in `apps/web/lib/analytics/get-analytics.ts`, where it builds the `v4_count`, `v4_timeseries`, `v4_group_by_link_metadata`, or `v4_group_by` pipe and invokes it with `tinybirdParams`.
