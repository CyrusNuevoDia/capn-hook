In `apps/web/lib/middleware/link.ts`, the redirect path creates or reuses a `dub_id` click ID, then calls `recordClick(...)` in `waitUntil` for the selected redirect/rewrite branch. `apps/web/lib/tinybird/record-click.ts` deduplicates via `recordClickCache` by `domain + key + identityHash` and, on a miss, posts the click to Tinybird `dub_click_events`, increments link counts, publishes usage/events; the Redis key/1-hour TTL lives in `apps/web/lib/api/links/record-click-cache.ts`.
