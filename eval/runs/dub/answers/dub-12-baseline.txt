Connected Stripe integration webhooks are dispatched in `apps/web/app/(ee)/api/stripe/integration/webhook/route.ts`.

The actual Dub attribution happens in `webhook/customer-created.ts` and `webhook/customer-updated.ts` via `utils/create-new-customer.ts` for lead attribution, and in `webhook/checkout-session-completed.ts` plus `webhook/invoice-paid.ts` for sale attribution, with promo-code attribution shared in `webhook/utils/attribute-via-promotion-code-id.ts`.
