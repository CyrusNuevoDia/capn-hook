Due commissions are aggregated into payouts in [route.ts](/Users/knrz/Git/CyrusNuevoDia/capn-hook/eval/runs/work/dub-capn/apps/web/app/(ee)/api/cron/payouts/aggregate-due-commissions/route.ts): it groups pending due commissions by partner/program, creates or reuses pending `Payout`s, and attaches commissions to them.

Those payouts are then charged for processing in [process-payouts.ts](/Users/knrz/Git/CyrusNuevoDia/capn-hook/eval/runs/work/dub-capn/apps/web/app/(ee)/api/cron/payouts/process/process-payouts.ts), which marks eligible payouts `processing`, computes totals/fees, updates the invoice, and creates the Stripe `PaymentIntent`.
